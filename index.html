Gib Fly!

How Does This Work?

In today’s Gibraltan fast paced world, you’ve got places you need to be but not enough time in your jam packed schedule. Wouldn’t it be nice if there were a super fast air transportation service that changed the way you get around daily? Introducing Gib Fly, an innovative transportation service that helps people get to their destination faster and hassle-free. Getting started is as easy as tapping a button in our app.

Download The App

Head over to the app store and download the Gib Fly app. You’re just a few taps away from getting your fast flight to anywhere in Gibraltar.

Request A Taxi

We can get you there. Simply request a flight on the app and we'll connect you with an automated flying taxi within minutes.

Pick A Price

Pick the valuation you're willing to pay and your flight is set up. The only surge is the acceleration you get when taking off.

Fly Off To Success!

Once you have boarded and your Qr code matches the taxi - you are away!

Our Story

Gib Fly was started by a former Gibraltar University student and a software developer. The two long-time friends happened upon the Gib Fly idea after attending a silent yoga retreat in Sotogrande. After realising they would need to come back on the AP-7 and hit on the idea of an automated flying taxi service to and from Gibraltar. The fact that it might be a bit dangerous was no obstacle. Modern electric technology was at hand. Fly with confidence with Gib Fly
